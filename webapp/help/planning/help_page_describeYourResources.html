Describe your resources

On this page you can enter some information about your own resources going into the project: your own skills, plus the help you will be able to rely on as you carry out the project.

What to do here

Describe your skills and resources at the start of your project.

Connections to other pages

The descriptions you enter here (along with your description of participant groups) will create the recommendations you see on these pages:

  • story collection venues
  • story eliciting questions
  • questions about stories
  • questions about participants
  • story collection sessions
  • sensemaking sessions

If you don't want to use NarraFirma's recommendation system, you can skip this page.

Frequently-asked questions

How much is "a lot?"

It doesn't matter, as long as you remember what it means to you when you look at the recommendations that are generated based on your answers here.
